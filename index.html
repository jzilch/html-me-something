Geocaching

Geocaching is the recreational activity of hunting for and finding a hidden object by means of GPS coordinates post on a community website. Geocaching is my active hoppy. Me and my girlfriend love geocaching on nice summer days. We both enjoy hiking and outdoors, so geocaching is a fun way for us to familiarize ourself with the landscape and new areas and parks we have never been too. The joy is finding hidden "caches" in the trees, logs, swamps, rocks, tall grass, and many more places. You get some coordinates and some clues to track them down. When you find the "cache" there is sometimes paper to write your name on to show you've been there and little trinkets that you swap with trinkets you have.

Anyone can make their own cache and include anything they want inside. Which introduces trackables. Trackables are trinkets that one places inside their cache and try to get it to another location. One trackable I just found started its journey in Hawaii and now is in St. Louis on its way across the US.
